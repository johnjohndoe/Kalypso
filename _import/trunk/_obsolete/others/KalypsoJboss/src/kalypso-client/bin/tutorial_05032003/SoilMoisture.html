Initial Conditions-Parameters



Initial conditions define hydrological conditions at the starting date of simulation. When running a longterm simulation Kalypso RRM can generate initial conditions for starting date of following shortterm simulation.
For this project initial conditions need to be estimated for the hydrological condition dependent on exiting climate (precipitation and temperature) and hydrological data. Main initial conditions for upper river basin Tisza are:
  • Soil moisture
  • Snow height
The other explained parameters can be fixed in model because of less influence.
The initial conditions are defined for a catchment.
User can define special state for initial conditions to save their validated modelversions for next simulations of flood events with same former conditions.

Winter with frozen soil
Kalypso RRM is not able to consider process of frozen soil regime. Special hydrological knowledge of climate situation is necessary.
Either frozen soil can be simulated by low infiltration rate or by changes of soil moisture which considers the frozen water in soillayer.
